Prediction markets · Data tooling

The probability of everything, where you work.

ManticView streams live probabilities from Manifold's prediction markets into the tools you already use — spreadsheet cells that recalculate and home-screen widgets that chart the future while you go about your day.

Two surfaces. One stream of foresight.

The same public Manifold data, shaped for the place you'll actually look at it.

In Google Sheets

Custom functions that treat probabilities like any other number — chart them, average them, alert on them.

=MANIFOLD_PROB("will-x-happen-by-2030") → 0.72 =MANIFOLD("will-x-happen-by-2030", "closeTime") → 12/31/2030 =MANIFOLD_ANSWERS("who-wins-the-final") → spills answers × probabilities

Built like an instrument.

Small, honest, and precise — the way good data tools should be.

Δ 1H–ALL

Honest comparison periods

Deltas are interpolated at the exact cutoff — "1H" means one hour, and the chart window follows.

{ GROUPS }

Watchlists & categories

Organize markets into groups; reorder, rename, or hide them from the widget entirely.

A | B | C

Every answer, separately

Track several answers of the same multiple-choice market side by side, each with its own chart.

WON / LOST

Resolution, resolved

Outcomes show in plain color-coded language, linger 48 hours, then auto-archive.

Light & dark

System, light, or dark — one setting themes the app and both widgets.
