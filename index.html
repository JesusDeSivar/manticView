Mantic View

Manifold prediction markets, directly in Google Sheets. Track probabilities, spill answer tables, and search markets with simple spreadsheet functions.

Get the template

=MANIFOLD_PROB()

The live probability of any YES/NO market as a plain number, ready to chart, average, or format as a percentage.

=MANIFOLD()

Any market attribute: question, close date, volume, resolution and more. Paste a market URL or just its slug.

=MANIFOLD_ANSWERS()

Multiple-choice markets spill into a tidy table of answers and probabilities, one row per outcome.

=MANIFOLD_SEARCH()

Search all of Manifold without leaving your sheet and get back questions, probabilities and links.
